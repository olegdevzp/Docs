# Capital.com Senior Developer HR Interview Preparation Guide

## 🚀 Company Snapshot
*   **Industry:** Fintech / Online Trading (Forex, Stocks, Crypto, Indices).
*   **Mission:** To make trading accessible, engaging, and useful with AI-powered tools.
*   **Key Tech Focus:** High reliability, low latency, AI/ML integration, user-friendly UX.
*   **Offices:** London, Warsaw, Limassol, Dubai, etc. (Global mindset).
*   **Vibe:** Fast-paced, "Start-up like" agility but with established backing.

---

## 💎 Core Values (The "Capital.com DNA")
*Memorize these and link your stories to them.*

1.  **Care:** Empathy for users and colleagues. (Focus on: Mentorship, User Experience, Code Quality).
2.  **Be Clear:** Transparency and direct communication. (Focus on: Handling conflict, Explaining technical concepts).
3.  **Be Bold:** Taking calculated risks, challenging the status quo. (Focus on: Innovation, Disagreeing with management).
4.  **Explore and Discover:** Curiosity, learning new tech. (Focus on: Staying current, continuous learning).
5.  **Move Fast:** Agility, shipping quickly. (Focus on: Managing deadlines, MVP mindset).
6.  **Focus on Outcomes:** Results over process. (Focus on: Impact, metrics, solving business problems).

---

## 🎯 Strategy: Linking Answers to Values

For a Senior role, they are looking for **Technical Excellence + Cultural Driver**.

### 1. "Why Capital.com?"
**Bad Answer:** "I need a job and I like fintech."
**Capital.com Answer:** "I admire how Capital.com combines complex financial data with a user-centric, AI-driven experience (**Explore & Discover**). I thrive in environments that prioritize agility (**Move Fast**) but don't compromise on the end result (**Focus on Outcomes**). I want to work on systems where reliability translates directly to user trust."

### 2. "Tell me about a time you failed."
*   **Map to:** **Focus on Outcomes** & **Be Clear**.
*   **Strategy:** Show you owned it immediately (transparency), fixed it quickly, and put systems in place to prevent recurrence. In Fintech, a failure often means money lost, so emphasize *safety mechanisms*.

### 3. "How do you handle tight deadlines?"
*   **Map to:** **Move Fast** & **Be Bold**.
*   **Strategy:** "I believe in moving fast by being bold enough to cut scope, not quality. If we have a tight deadline, I negotiate the 'Must Haves' to ensure we ship value quickly without introducing critical bugs (Debt) that would slow us down later."

### 4. "How do you handle disagreement?"
*   **Map to:** **Care** & **Be Clear**.
*   **Strategy:** "I focus on being clear and direct but caring. I attack the problem, not the person. I use data to support my point, but once a decision is made, I commit fully to the team's success."

---

## 🏦 Fintech/Capital.com Specific Considerations

Since this is a trading platform, you should be ready for questions related to:
*   **High Availability:** "How do you ensure 99.99% uptime?" (Crucial for trading).
*   **Concurrency:** Handling thousands of price updates per second.
*   **Accuracy:** Floating point math issues (0.1 + 0.2 !== 0.3), exactness in financial calculations.
*   **Regulation:** Understanding that sometimes "Technical Constraints" come from "Legal Constraints" (KYC, AML).

---

## ❓ Questions to Ask Them (Impress the HR)

1.  "I know Capital.com values **'Moving Fast'**. How does the engineering team balance that speed with the strict reliability requirements of a financial platform?"
2.  "With the **'Explore and Discover'** value, how does the company support developers who want to experiment with new technologies like AI or Rust in production?"
3.  "How does the team embody the **'Care'** value when it comes to code reviews and mentorship? Is there a formal structure for seniors to support juniors?"
4.  "Capital.com has grown rapidly. What is the biggest cultural challenge the engineering team is facing right now as it scales?"
