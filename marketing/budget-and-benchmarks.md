# Mobile App Marketing Budget & Benchmarks

> How much money people usually spend on marketing for mobile apps.

There is no single "usual" number — mobile app marketing spend varies enormously by app type, competition, and growth goals. Below is a practical breakdown based on current industry data (2024–2025).

---

## The Short Answer

| Who | Typical First-Year Marketing Spend |
|-----|-------------------------------------|
| **Indie / solo** | **$2k–$15k** (often mostly organic + ASO) |
| **Small startup** | **$15k–$100k** |
| **Funded startup (growth mode)** | **$100k–$500k+** |
| **Scale-up / competitive category** | **$500k– millions** |

### Rule of Thumb

Plan for **30–50% of your total first-year app investment** to go to marketing — not just development.

**Example:** If the app cost $50k to build, many teams budget **$15k–$25k** for year-one marketing.

---

## What the Market Looks Like (2024–2025)

- **Global app install ad spend** is projected around **~$95 billion in 2025** (AppsFlyer).
- Roughly **two-thirds of app marketers** reported **larger budgets in 2025** vs. the prior year.
- Most users only regularly use **~10 apps per day**, so paid acquisition is often necessary to break through.

---

## Monthly Spend Ranges

For paid growth with real visibility:

| Level | Monthly spend |
|-------|---------------|
| Minimum meaningful paid spend | ~$3k–$10k/month |
| Typical startup growth budget | $10k–$50k/month |
| Competitive categories (fintech, dating, games, US market) | $50k–$100k+/month |

### Budget Timeline

- **Pre-launch:** 10–15% of annual marketing budget
- **First 1–3 months after launch:** 40–50% of annual budget (early traction matters most)
- **After quarter two:** Monthly spend stabilizes as organic growth increases

## What You're Actually Paying For

### Pre-Launch

| Item | Cost range |
|------|------------|
| Market research | $5k–$15k |
| Beta testing | up to ~$5k |
| ASO tools | $25–$1,500/month |
| PR | $100–$300/hour |
| Influencer campaigns (agency-managed) | $10k–$18k+ |

### Launch Campaigns

- **$5k–$100k+** depending on category and ambition

### Ongoing Retention

| Item | Cost range |
|------|------------|
| Push / in-app messaging | $1k–$5k/month |
| Analytics | $500–$2k/month |
| Re-engagement campaigns | $5k–$30k/month |

Many teams also allocate **20–30%** of marketing budget to retention, not just new installs.

### Other Line Items

| Item | Cost range |
|------|------------|
| App marketing agency pricing | $1k–$25k |
| App launch campaigns | $5k–$100k |
| App personalization and gamification | $2k–$10k |
| User engagement campaigns | $5k–$30k |

## Cost Per Install (CPI) — The Metric That Drives Spend

If you know CPI and how many users you want:

```
Budget = installs × CPI
```

### Channel Benchmarks (Approximate Averages)

| Channel | Avg CPI |
|---------|---------|
| Apple Search Ads | ~$1.42 |
| Google Ads | ~$2.65 |
| Unity Ads | ~$2.50 |
| ironSource | ~$2.75 |
| TikTok | ~$2.88 |
| AppLovin / Vungle | ~$3.00 |
| Instagram | ~$3.50 |
| Facebook | ~$3.75 |

### Platform Averages

- **iOS:** ~$3.60 globally
- **Android:** ~$1.20 globally

Gaming, finance, and dating can be **$5–$15+** per install.

### Example

10,000 installs at $3 CPI ≈ **$30,000** in ad spend alone (before creative, tools, or agency fees).

## Realistic Scenarios

### 1. Bootstrapped Indie App

- **Spend:** $0–$5k total
- **Tactics:** ASO, social content, Product Hunt, Reddit
- **Outcome:** Growth is slow unless the app is inherently viral

### 2. Small Business App

- **Spend:** $15k–$50k first year
- **Tactics:** ASO + modest paid ads + basic retention tools

### 3. VC-Backed Consumer App

- **Spend:** $200k–$1M+ first year
- **Tactics:** Heavy paid UA, influencers, PR, retention stack

### 4. Mobile Game

- Often **50–80%+ of revenue** goes back into UA
- Some studios spend **millions per month**

---

## Practical Budgeting Formula

```
Year 1 marketing budget ≈
  (Target installs × CPI)
  + ASO/tools ($3k–$20k)
  + Launch PR/creatives ($5k–$50k)
  + Retention stack ($12k–$60k/year)
```

### Unit Economics Check

**Can one user pay back their CPI within ~90 days?**

If CPI is $4 and LTV is $2, paid marketing will burn cash quickly.

## 12-Month Budget Snapshot (Solo, Bootstrapped)

| Tier | Year 1 Spend | Expected Outcome |
|------|--------------|------------------|
| **$0** | Domain only | 500–2,000 installs if niche + consistent content |
| **$2k–$5k** | ASO tools, small ads, Canva, email | 2,000–10,000 installs |
| **$10k–$20k** | Ads + freelance ASO/creative help | 10,000–50,000 installs (category dependent) |

---

## Bottom Line

- **"Usual" is very wide:** from a few thousand dollars (organic-first) to **$10k–$50k/month** for serious startup growth.
- **First-year marketing often equals 30–50% of total app investment.**
- **Launch is the most expensive phase**; retention should be **20–30%** of ongoing spend.
- Budget from **target users × CPI**, not from a generic percentage alone.
